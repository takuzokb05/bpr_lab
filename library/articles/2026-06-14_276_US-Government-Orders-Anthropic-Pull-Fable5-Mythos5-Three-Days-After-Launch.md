## 投稿内容
Federal government orders Anthropic to pull Fable 5 and Mythos 5, three days after launch. Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick issued the directive to Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei. Fable 5 launched June 9 with SWE-Bench Pro 80.3% (11 points ahead of competition), but was taken offline June 12 citing cyberattack information risk from jailbreaks. Amazon CEO Andy Jassy contacted Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent on June 12 about findings. The New Stack analysis covers the technical and policy implications.
