## 投稿内容

Cat Wu, head of product for Claude Code at Anthropic:
"you can build almost anything with Claude from a single prompt now"

And she's right. But one prompt gets you a working thing, not YOUR thing. Claude's never seen your taste, so it guesses fresh every time and hands you something that could've come from anyone.

Same deal with UGC video. One prompt gets you generic AI slop that looks like the other 10,000 clips in the feed. The hook, the pacing, the way you actually talk, that's the part that makes people stop scrolling, and it's the part Claude can't guess.

So you stop letting it guess. You hand it a file that holds your voice and your style, and now it scripts UGC that sounds like you wrote it, before you touch a thing.
