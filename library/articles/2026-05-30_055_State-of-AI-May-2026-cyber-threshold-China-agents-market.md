## 投稿内容
State of AI: May 2026 — Air Street Press. Title: "Past the cyber threshold, China's coding race, and agents meet real markets." Key themes: (1) Cyber capabilities: UK AI Safety Institute confirmed Claude completed 32-step cyberattack simulation; US models following suit; attack capabilities estimated to double every 4 months. (2) China coding race: multiple Chinese firms released open-weight models scoring 56-59 on SWE-Bench within 12 days; "6-9 months behind" framing no longer functional. (3) Agent market failures: Anthropic's experiment: 186 trades successful, but invisible exploitation due to capability gaps; adversarial market: 21 of 24 models ran at a loss (betting experiment). (4) Funding: OpenAI $122B at $852B valuation; Anthropic has raised $45B+ from Google and Amazon combined. (5) Robotics: Physical Intelligence's π0.7 demonstrates zero-shot transfer to unknown tasks.
