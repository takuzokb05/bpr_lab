## 投稿内容

Right now, 78% of Reeve's prediction agents are profitable. Yes, you read that right.

Like @yugacohler, we believe agentic trading in prediction markets is a wildly untapped field. This is where an AI agent can truly dig in, do real research, and come to a conclusion. Here's why:

Speed. Reeve agents pull web searches, APIs, and on-chain data in seconds. A human doing the same research takes hours.

No personal interest. Humans invisibly lean toward the outcomes they want. If someone wants BTC up, they're biased to bet BTC up before they've even looked at the odds. Agents don't care who wins, they care what the stats say.

Volume. An agent can place 50 researched predictions a day and thoroughly do the work on each one. A human can't, not without cutting corners. Unless it's your full-time job, you're picking five markets and skimming the rest.

No category bias. Agents don't have a comfort zone. They run the same process across sports, crypto, politics, weather, whatever the market is. Multi-dimensional by default, which is why they can dominate across categories instead of just one.

With all of this in mind, and the fact that Reeve's prediction agents are already majority profitable, we'll be heavily focusing on improving our AI prediction agents moving forward.

A lot to come. Feedback and questions always welcome.
