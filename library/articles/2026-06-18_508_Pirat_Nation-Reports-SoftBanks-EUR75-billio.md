SoftBank has announced plans to invest up to €75 billion (around $87 billion) in AI data centers across France, making it the largest AI infrastructure investment ever announced in Europe.

The project will create up to 5 gigawatts of AI computing capacity, with the first phase involving a €45 billion investment and several major data center sites in northern France.

If completed as planned, it will be one of the largest AI data center developments in the world
