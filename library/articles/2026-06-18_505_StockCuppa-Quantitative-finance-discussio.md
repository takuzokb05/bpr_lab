## 投稿内容

A new model that raises portfolio returns might boost Sharpe, but a new model that both raises portfolio returns while *lowering* MDD is going to boost Calmar.

Calmar is king is far as I'm concerned👑 Sortino isn't too far behind it though😌 A new model should boost everyone of these 3 measures imo.

However, to be quite honest I don't pay too much attention to these specific numbers the AI spits out. It can fudge things around and reach different answers depending on that session's context. Sometimes it just wants to flatter you with the most generous answer.

I look at the holistic picture of all the results a new models gives me throughout testing, between screening in Python, MT5 strategy testing, and prompting the AI for numbers with a request to be conservative and double-checking itself.

I pay special attention to the manual MT5 strategy tester results (new model executions crowding existing models, remaining <2% MDD over the full term at 0.25% risk per trade), and the Monte Carlo simulation later.

If numbers do finally start to find parity between different instruments, then that paints for me an overall picture of whether I'm seeing the new model with mostly signal or too much noise.
