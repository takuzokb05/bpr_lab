## 投稿内容

Apple’s camera equipped AI AirPods are now expected to launch in late 2027 🚨

Internally codenamed B798, these AirPods are being built as Apple’s first AI-first wearable.

Instead of taking photos or videos, the tiny cameras embedded in the stems will act as computer vision sensors, feeding real-time visual context to Siri so it can understand what you’re looking at.

Imagine pointing your head at ingredients in your kitchen and asking “What can I cook?” or getting contextual reminders, object recognition, and smarter walking directions without pulling out your iPhone.

The launch was originally planned for 2026, but reportedly slipped to late 2027 due to Apple’s ongoing AI software challenges and the need to develop reliable visual AI models capable of understanding a user’s surroundings.

Apple is also exploring indicator lights that notify people nearby whenever visual data is being sent to the cloud for processing.

The project is part of Apple’s broader Visual Intelligence strategy and its push into AI wearables ahead of its upcoming smart glasses.
