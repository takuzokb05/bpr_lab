yeah i’ts right that the marginal cost is absurd, but we have a little problem 

$0.25/M output is the FLOOR, max batch, high latency, short context. that’s literally why deepseek runs slow: they trade latency for batch size to hit that number

blended margin says the opposite. anthropic guided ~40% gross margin for 2025, down from a 50% target. openai went from 40% to 33% and burned $9B last year.

where’s the spread going? ~95% of openai’s users are free / reasoning tokens the lab eats internally / KV cache on long context / overprovisioning to keep latency low.

the 90% number only exists if you serve paying API at max batch and train nothing. nobody runs like that. the spread isn’t profit, it’s the cross-subsidy paying for your free tier and the next training run
