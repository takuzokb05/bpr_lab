## 投稿内容

Anthropic studied 400,000 Claude Code sessions. Here's what they found.

Domain expertise beats coding ability.

A lawyer who knows exactly what they need succeeds at nearly the same rate as a software engineer.

Experts get 5x more output per prompt than novices.
Not because of better prompts. Because they know what they're asking for.

And the share of sessions spent debugging fell by nearly half in 7 months.

AI isn't replacing coders. but it is making domain knowledge the most valuable thing in the room.
