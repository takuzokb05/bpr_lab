## 投稿内容
Google has officially announced its new "Googlebook" laptops, replacing Chromebooks.

- Built around Gemini AI with deep system-wide integration  
- "Magic Pointer" lets users trigger Gemini actions just by hovering over on-screen elements  
- Run Android apps natively and access phone files seamlessly  
- Acer, ASUS, Dell, HP, and Lenovo will launch the first models later this year
