## 投稿内容
I’ve been waiting for this launch from @InterlaceMoney.

The reason is simple:

AI agents are getting smarter, but letting them spend real money still feels risky.

That is why Interlace Agent Card caught my attention.

It gives agents access to real Visa and Mastercard rails, but the important part is not just “spending”.

The important part is control:

• Task-based spending limits  
• Whitelisted merchants only  
• Category rules for tools, APIs, data, or SaaS  
• Time windows when I can actually monitor activity  
• JIT funding, so the card stays at zero until a valid transaction is approved  

For my own agents, I would never give them unlimited access to funds.

I would rather give them clear rules, small limits, and permission only when the task really needs it.

This is what makes AI agents feel more practical to me:

Not just agents that can think.

Agents that can act, pay, and still stay inside safe boundaries.
