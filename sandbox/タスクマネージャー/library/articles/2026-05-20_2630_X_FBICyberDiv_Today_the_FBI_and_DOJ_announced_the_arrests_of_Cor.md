## 投稿内容
Today the FBI and DOJ announced the arrests of Cornelius Shannon and Arturo Hernandez, both of whom were charged with violations of the TAKE IT DOWN Act. The TAKE IT DOWN Act prohibits nonconsensual publication of AI-generated digital forgeries (deepfake) pornography. Shannon and Hernandez allegedly posted thousands of images and videos that appeared to depict real people nude and/or engaging in sexual acts, and victims included actresses, singers, and political figures.
