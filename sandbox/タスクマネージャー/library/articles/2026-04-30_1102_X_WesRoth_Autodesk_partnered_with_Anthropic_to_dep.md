## 投稿内容
Autodesk partnered with Anthropic to deploy a native Claude connector directly into Autodesk Fusion, powered by the new Model Context Protocol (MCP).

This update bridges the gap between conversational AI and complex 3D design. 

Users can now tap into Claude’s advanced desktop capabilities including vision, speech recognition, web search, and cross-service workflows right inside their active manufacturing and design environment.
