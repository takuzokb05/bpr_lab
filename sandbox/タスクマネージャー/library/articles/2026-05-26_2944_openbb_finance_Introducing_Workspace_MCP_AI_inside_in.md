## 投稿内容
Introducing Workspace MCP 🎉 AI inside investment firms has moved past chat interfaces.

The firms moving fastest are running long-running agents, Claude Code, Codex, and similar systems that are built to run complex workflows end to end, but still lack the infrastructure to do it inside a financial firm.

Most of that work ends up ungoverned. Outputs land in chat threads and local folders. No lineage, no access controls, no clear path into production. Some CTOs are hiring people just to review what the analysts' agents produced.

Today we're introducing Workspace MCP.

It gives any MCP-compatible agent the infrastructure it needs to operate inside your firm: your data sources, your entitlements, your security perimeter. Agents can run portfolio risk assessments, build apps from connected data, explain existing dashboards, and push analysis directly into shareable widgets.

The work gets done. And everyone can trace it, trust it, and build on top of it.

Available now for all users.
