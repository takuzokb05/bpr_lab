At I/O, we announced a big new export partner: Bolt by StackBlitz! ⚡️

@boltdotnew is an AI-powered builder that generates full-stack web apps directly in your browser. Now, you can take your Stitch screens from a visual concept to a fully coded application with a single click.

Here is how it works:

🔹 Select your screens in Stitch
🔹 Click export
🔹 Your screens are automatically transferred to Bolt, ready to be built into a working product

🛠️ (Note: Bolt will accept up to 5 screens at a time for Bolt Free users, and up to 20 screens for Paid users)
