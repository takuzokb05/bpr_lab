## 投稿内容

Google just announced the biggest upgrade to Search in over 25 years 🚨

- Gemini 3.5 Flash is now the default model powering AI Mode
- New search box designed for longer, natural language queries
- Upload documents, photos, videos and even Chrome tabs directly into Search
- Smart suggestions help you build more complex questions
- AI Overviews and AI Mode now work together more seamlessly
- New agents can monitor topics and bring you updates later
-  Search can book services and even call businesses on your behalf
- Custom widgets, tools and mini apps are coming to Search
- More interactive AI-powered experiences are coming directly to results

This is CRAZY!

## 要約

Google I/OにてGoogle SearchがGemini 3.5 Flashをデフォルトモデルとして採用し、25年来最大のアップグレードを発表。自然言語対応の新検索ボックス、文書・動画・Chromeタブの直接アップロード対応、トピック監視エージェント、サービス予約・企業への代理電話など、AIエージェント機能が大幅強化された。AI OverviewsとAI Modeの統合も進む。
