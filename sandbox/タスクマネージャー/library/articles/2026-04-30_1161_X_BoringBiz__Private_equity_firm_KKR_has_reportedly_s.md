## 投稿内容
Private equity firm KKR has reportedly secured over $10 billion to launch a new company that will develop and operate AI infrastructure

The company will be called Helix Digital Infrastructure and will partner with hyperscalers to operate assets such as data centers, power generations and transmission

Former CEO of AWS, Adam Selipsky, will be running the business with KKR’s global head of digital infrastructure, Waldemar Szlezak, acting as CIO
