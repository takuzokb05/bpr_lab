## 投稿内容
xAI has just rolled out Connectors, a powerful new feature that turns Grok into a true AI agent capable of securely integrating with your everyday tools and apps. 

Connectors use the Model Context Protocol (MCP) — an open standard (originally pioneered by Anthropic and now widely adopted) that lets AI models safely connect to external services. 

Think of it as a universal "plug" for your data and tools. Grok can now pull real-time information, summarize content, automate tasks, and act across platforms without you copying/pasting everything manually.

There are several other options just from these, and also users can add random connector for their account.
