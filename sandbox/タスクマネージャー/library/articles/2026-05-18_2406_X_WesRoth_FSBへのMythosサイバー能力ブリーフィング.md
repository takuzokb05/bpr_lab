## 投稿内容
Anthropic has agreed to brief the Financial Stability Board, whose members include G20 finance ministries, central banks, and securities regulators, about the capabilities of Claude Mythos Preview. 

The concern is that Mythos can uncover serious cybersecurity vulnerabilities, which could help defenders patch systems faster but could also raise systemic risk if similar capabilities are misused against financial institutions.
