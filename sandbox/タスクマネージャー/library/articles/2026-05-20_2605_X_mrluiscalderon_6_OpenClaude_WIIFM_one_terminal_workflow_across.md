6/ OpenClaude

WIIFM: one terminal workflow across model providers.

Pros: local/cloud providers, MCP, tools, agents, VS Code hooks.
Cons: fast-moving legal/compatibility edge, expect sharp corners.

Try if: you want Claude Code-style workflows without one-provider lock-in.
