SpaceX just filed their S-1. 

A few numbers worth knowing:

- $18.7B in 2025 revenue
- Starlink alone: $11.4B at a 63% EBITDA margin
- Anthropic is paying them $1.25B/month for compute
- AI capex: $12.7B last year, $7.7B in Q1 alone
- TAM claim: $28.5 trillion

Everyone's going to call this a rocket IPO. The filing says it's an AI compute and connectivity company that also launches rockets.
