## 投稿内容
What they MISSED:

1. No image or vision support. DeepSeek's endpoint does not handle multimodal input.
2. No MCP server tools. The compatibility layer cannot bridge that gap.
3. No Anthropic prompt caching savings. DeepSeek has its own caching but it is different.

The deeper miss: reliability at the tail. When your agent hits an ambiguous error at step 15, model quality determines whether it recovers or spirals.
