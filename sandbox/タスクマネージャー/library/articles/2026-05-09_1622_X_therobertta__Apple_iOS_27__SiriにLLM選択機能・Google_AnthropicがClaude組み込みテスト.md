Apple is building "Extensions" into iOS 27 that let users choose which LLM powers Siri, Writing Tools, and Image Playground. Google and Anthropic are already being tested.

Most people see this as Apple playing catch-up on AI. They are wrong. Apple just turned the iPhone into the first consumer agent harness. The models are the swappable engines. iOS is the chassis. And this changes everything about how AI reaches people.
