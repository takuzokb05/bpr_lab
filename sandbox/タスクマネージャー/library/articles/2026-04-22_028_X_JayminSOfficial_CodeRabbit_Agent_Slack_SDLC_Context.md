## 投稿内容
CodeRabbit just made a meaningful move beyond AI code review with the launch of @coderabbitai Agent for Slack.

AI has already reshaped how developers write code. Output is faster, iteration cycles are shorter, and individual productivity continues to improve. Yet the core challenge in engineering has remained largely unchanged, how teams think, decide, and execute together across the SDLC.

Context lives in scattered tools. Decisions stay buried in Slack threads. Knowledge fades between planning, coding, testing, and deployment. Every phase starts with partial visibility, even in high performing teams.

Built on 2M+ weekly PR reviews and 15,000+ teams, CodeRabbit is now addressing this structural gap by turning everyday workflows into a continuous system of context and memory. The agent captures decisions as they happen, connects them across tools, and makes them accessible at any point in the lifecycle.

What makes this interesting is the shift in focus. From accelerating individual tasks to strengthening collective intelligence.
