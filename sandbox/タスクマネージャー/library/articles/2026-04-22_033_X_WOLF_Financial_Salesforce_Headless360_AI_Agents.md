SALESFORCE CO-FOUNDER PARKER HARRIS: "WHY SHOULD YOU EVER LOG INTO SALESFORCE AGAIN?"

The company $CRM just announced its biggest architectural overhaul in 27 years. It's called "Headless 360" and the idea is simple: AI agents run the entire platform. No browser needed.

What shipped:

• 100+ new tools giving AI coding agents full access to the Salesforce platform

• Works with Claude, GPT-5, Gemini, and any coding agent

• AI agents can now build, deploy, and manage Salesforce apps from any terminal

• Moving from per-seat licensing to consumption-based pricing

The pricing shift tells you everything. When AI agents do the work instead of humans, charging per user doesn't make sense anymore.

One customer built a service agent in 12 days. It now handles 50% of cases on its own.
