## 投稿内容
Anthropic built Mythos: found thousands of zero-days across every major OS and browser. So dangerous they won't release it publicly. Committed $100M in usage credits to defensive security efforts.

OpenAI built Daybreak: frontier AI for cyber defenders, combining their models with Codex.
On paper these look like competing products. They're not.

Mythos is a research finding that something crossed a threshold. Daybreak is a product. One is Anthropic saying "this capability exists and it's serious enough that we're treating it as a coordinated global security event." The other is OpenAI saying "here's a tool for your security team."

Very different levels of alarm. Worth paying attention to which one is more scared of what they built.
