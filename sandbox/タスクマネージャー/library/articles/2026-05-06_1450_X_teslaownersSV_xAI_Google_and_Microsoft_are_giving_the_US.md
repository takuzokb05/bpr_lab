## 投稿内容
xAI, Google and Microsoft are giving the U.S. early access to new AI models.

The companies will share their latest systems with the government for preliminary national security risk checks before public release. It’s a coordinated move to get ahead of potential misuse and capability risks.

They’re working with CAISI on early evaluations, and more than 40 assessments have already been completed. The focus is on what these models can do and where they might create security concerns.

This signals a shift toward tighter collaboration between AI labs and government as models get more powerful.
