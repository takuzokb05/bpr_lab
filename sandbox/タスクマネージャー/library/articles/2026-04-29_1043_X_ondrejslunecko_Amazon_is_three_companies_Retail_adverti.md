Amazon is three companies. Retail, advertising, and cloud. Two of them are world-class. One of them funds the other two. $AMZN

The $15.6B in "other income" this quarter, largely from Anthropic investment gains, inflated net income significantly. Strip that out and operating income tells the cleaner story: still up 30%.

AWS keeps printing. That's what matters.

Key metrics Q1 FY25 → Q1 FY26:

Total net sales: $155,667M → $181,519M (+17%)
Operating income: $18,405M → $23,852M (+30%)
Net income: $17,127M → $30,255M (+77%)*
EPS (basic): $1.62 → $2.82 (+74%)

*Includes ~$15.6B investment gains
