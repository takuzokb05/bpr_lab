## 投稿内容
ANTHROPIC JUST ANNOUNCED A MAJOR FINANCIAL SERVICES PUSH WITH 10 NEW AI AGENT TEMPLATES AND CLAUDE INTEGRATION ACROSS MICROSOFT 365

Announced today, per Anthropic:

The 10 new agent templates target the most time-consuming work in financial services:
- Research and client coverage: pitch builder, meeting preparer, earnings reviewer, model builder
- Credit, risk, and compliance: market researcher, KYC screener
- Finance and operations: valuation reviewer, general ledger reconciler, month-end closer, statement auditor

Claude is now integrated across Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, Word, and Outlook (coming soon) via add-ins. Context carries automatically between applications: an analyst who starts a model in Excel doesn't need to re-explain it when the work moves to PowerPoint.

The competitive claim: Anthropic's Claude Opus 4.7 leads the industry on Vals AI's Finance Agent benchmark at 64.37%.

New data partner connectors: Dun & Bradstreet, Fiscal AI, Financial Modeling Prep, Guidepoint, IBISWorld, SS&C IntraLinks, Third Bridge, and Verisk. Moody's also launched an MCP app providing access to credit ratings on 600+ million public and private companies.

The launch comes the same day Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei told a New York finance event that the company's Q1 revenue grew "80x" on an annualized basis and warned individual SaaS companies "may go bust from AI disruption."
